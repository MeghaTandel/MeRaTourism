Cultural Heritage & Historical Sites Tour

Key Attractions:

  • Rani ki Vav (Ranki Vaav): A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its intricate stepwell architecture.
  • Patan Museum: Houses ancient artifacts, bronze statues, and artifacts from the Solanki dynasty.
  • Shastraling Talav (Sahastralinga Talav): An ancient water tank with historical and religious significance.
  • Veer Megh Maya Tekri: A hilltop temple dedicated to Saint Veer Meghmaya.
