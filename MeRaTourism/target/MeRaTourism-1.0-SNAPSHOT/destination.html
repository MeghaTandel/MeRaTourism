Patan Destinations

Ranki Vaav

Ranki Vaav is an intricately constructed stepwell situated in Patan, Gujarat.

Patan Museum

Patan Museum showcases the rich history and culture of Patan.

Khan Sarovar

Khan Sarovar is a beautiful lake located in Patan, perfect for a peaceful visit.

Sahastraling Talav

Sahastraling Talav is an ancient water reservoir with historical significance.

Government Engineering College Patan

Government Engineering College Patan is a prestigious educational institution.

Dinosaur Park

Dinosaur Park is a fun and educational destination for all ages.

Patola House

Patola House is famous for its exquisite Patola sarees.

Veer Megh Maya Tekri

Veer Megh Maya Tekri is a scenic hill offering stunning views.

Palika Bajar

Palika Bajar is a bustling market with a variety of shops and stalls.
